# Privacy Policy for EGX Portfolio Tracker

**Last updated: [DATE]**

## Introduction

EGX Portfolio Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally
- **Portfolio Data**: Stock holdings, transactions, dividends, and investment data you enter
- **User Preferences**: App settings and preferences
- All data is stored **locally on your device** using Android's Room database

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do **not** collect personal information
- We do **not** collect usage analytics
- We do **not** collect device identifiers
- We do **not** share your data with third parties

## Third-Party Services

### Stock Price APIs
Our app fetches stock prices from:
- **TradingView API**: Used to get real-time EGX stock prices
- **CNBC API**: Used as a fallback source for stock prices

**What we send**: Only stock symbols (e.g., "COMI", "ABUK")
**What we receive**: Current stock prices and market data
**Data sharing**: We do not share your portfolio data with these services

## Data Storage

- All your portfolio data is stored **locally on your device**
- Data is **not** synced to cloud services
- Data is **not** backed up to our servers
- If you uninstall the app, all data is deleted

## Data Security

- We use Android's built-in security features
- Local database is encrypted by Android's file system encryption
- No data transmission occurs except for fetching public stock prices

## Your Rights

- You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
- You can export your data (feature may be added in future updates)
- You control all data stored in the app

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
